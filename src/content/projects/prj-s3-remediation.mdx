## Context
Organisation-wide uplift of S3 bucket security posture by rolling out baseline controls consistently.

## Baseline Controls (high level)
- Encryption enforcement (at-rest requirements)
- Secure transport enforcement (HTTPS)
- Policy drift minimisation via IaC rollout patterns

## Rollout Strategy
- Discovery and classification
- Staged rollout with guardrails
- Verification and reporting

## Outcomes
- Reduced audit risk and inconsistency
- Repeatable, governed storage baseline
